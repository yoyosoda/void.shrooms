Rules

  • You must like fungi in some form or fashion.
  • No NFTs or crypto currency.
  • NSFW and/or suggestive content is allowed if marked as such.
  • You must be 18 or older. (Why? Look a the above rule.)
  • There will be no tolarance for hate speech or bigotry.
  • Have a website that you're able to add a widget to. No social media.
  • A dark/gothic aesthetic for your site is not required but appreciated.
  • We reserve the right to remove or reject a site if it is outside the webring's vision.

Add yourself to the members list

Add a unique slug, your webpage's name, and your webpage's URL to the members list and your button (if you have one) to the button folder on GitHub in a pull request.

An example of how to add you're to the member list:

slug name url desc button nickname fungi nsfw
[Your slug] "[Your Site Name]" [Site URL] "[Your site description]" "[The file name of your button (leave blank if you don't have one)]" "[Your name]" "[Your favorite fungi]" ["y" for NSFW, "n" for SFW]
void "Void.Shrooms" https://void.shroom.ink "A webring for fungi lovers who enjoy dark/gothic aesthetics!" "void.shrooms.jpg" "Void.Shrooms Webmaster" "Devil's Tooth" n
